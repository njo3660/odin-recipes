Hash Browns

Description

Hash browns are the perfect side dish for any breakfast and they're easier to make than you might think!

Ingredients

  • 2 russet potatoes, shredded
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
